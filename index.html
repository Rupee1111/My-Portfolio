About Me
Design is how it works,
not just how it looks.

I'm a UI/UX Designer based in Jaipur, India, with a background in Information Technology and a deep passion for crafting digital experiences that feel effortless to use. I believe great design starts with understanding people — their goals, frustrations, and context.

Over the past 2+ years I've worked across agencies and product companies, designing mobile apps, SaaS dashboards, and e-commerce experiences. My process blends research rigor with visual craft — from rough wireframes through polished, dev-ready Figma files.

When I'm not designing, I'm exploring interaction patterns, studying great apps, and continuously leveling up my design system skills.

My Design Process
01
Discover & Research
User interviews, competitor analysis, and usability studies to uncover real problems worth solving.
02
Define & Structure
User journeys, personas, and information architecture to establish clear product direction.
03
Design & Prototype
Wireframes through high-fidelity Figma designs, with interactive prototypes for testing.
04
Test & Iterate
Usability testing, feedback synthesis, and iteration — then pixel-perfect developer handoff.
